Malcolm X, born Malcolm Little, and later also known as el-Hajj Malik el-Shabazz.

Who is Malcolm X?

An African-American Muslim minister and human rights activist. A courageous advocate for the rights of blacks, a man who indicted white America in the harshest terms for its crimes against black Americans. He has been called one of the greatest and most influential African Americans in history.
